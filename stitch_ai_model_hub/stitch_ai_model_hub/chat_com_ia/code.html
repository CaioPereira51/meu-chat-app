Certainly! Designing for high-performance AI interfaces requires a focus on **visual hierarchy** and **information density**. To achieve a "Laboratory Clean" aesthetic with Indigo accents, we should prioritize tonal layering over high-contrast borders.

Core Design Principles

  • Tonal Depth: Use progressively lighter shades of slate to indicate elevation.
  • Accent Purpose: Limit Indigo (#494bd6) to active states and primary functional cues.
  • Negative Space: Ensure generous padding (24px+) for complex data sets.
